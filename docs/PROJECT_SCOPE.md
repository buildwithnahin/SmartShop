# SmartShop v1 Scope

## Objective

Build a production-structured full-stack mobile application for local business operations.

## In Scope (v1)

- User authentication with JWT
- Product management
- Customer management
- Sales creation with transaction safety
- Sales item management
- Daily and monthly reports
- Low stock alerts
- Product search
- Sales date filtering

## Out of Scope (v1.1+)

- Barcode scanning
- Product images
- Offline sync
- Multi-branch inventory
- Receipt printer integration

## User Roles

- `admin`: full access
- `staff`: limited operational access (exact permissions to be finalized)
